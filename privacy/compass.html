概述

FLB Studio（以下简称"我们"）重视您的个人隐私。本隐私政策说明了我们的 Compass 应用（以下简称"本应用"）在您使用期间如何收集、使用及保护相关信息。继续使用本应用即表示您已阅读并接受本政策。

我们收集的信息

设备传感器数据

Compass 依赖您设备内置的磁力计和加速度计提供实时方向指引。所有传感器数据仅在您的设备本地处理，不会通过网络传输或存储至任何外部服务器。

位置信息（可选）

若您选择授予位置权限，本应用将获取当地的磁偏角数值，以实现真北与磁北之间的精确切换。位置数据仅在当次会话期间临时使用，不会被持久保存或上传。您可随时在系统设置中撤销位置权限，罗盘的基本功能不受影响。

我们不收集的信息

  • 姓名、电子邮件地址或其他个人身份信息
  • 设备唯一标识符（IDFA、IDFV 或其他广告追踪 ID）
  • 使用习惯、行为分析或任何遥测数据
  • 任何经由网络传输的数据

如何使用信息

我们所获取的有限信息（传感器数据、可选位置数据）仅用于提供应用核心功能，不用于任何商业、广告或分析目的。

数据存储与安全

所有数据处理均在您的设备本地完成，本应用不向任何外部服务器发送数据。用户偏好设置（如是否启用真北）保存于 iOS/Android 系统的沙盒存储中，仅可由本应用访问，不可被其他应用读取。

信息共享

我们不会将您的任何信息出售、出租或以任何形式共享给第三方。

第三方服务

Compass 不集成任何第三方分析、广告或崩溃追踪 SDK。本应用通过 Apple App Store 或 Google Play 分发；您的账户及支付信息受 Apple 或 Google 各自的隐私政策约束，与我们无关。

您的权利

  • 撤回位置权限：随时前往「设置 > 隐私与安全 > 位置服务」修改授权
  • 删除数据：卸载本应用即可清除设备上所有本地数据
  • 提出疑问：您可通过下方联系方式与我们联系

儿童隐私

本应用不针对 13 岁以下（欧盟地区为 16 岁以下）的儿童。我们不会故意收集未成年人的个人信息。如您认为未成年人意外提供了个人信息，请立即联系我们，我们将予以处理。

隐私政策变更

我们可能因业务发展或法律合规需要不定期更新本政策。更新内容将在应用商店版本日志中注明；重大变更将至少提前 30 天通过应用内通知告知您。

适用法律

本政策受中华人民共和国相关法律法规管辖解释。

Overview

FLB Studio ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Compass app ("the App") collects, uses, and safeguards information during your use. By continuing to use the App, you acknowledge that you have read and accepted this policy.

Information We Collect

Device Sensor Data

Compass uses your device's built-in magnetometer and accelerometer to provide real-time directional guidance. All sensor data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted over the network or stored on any external server.

Location Information (Optional)

If you grant location permission, the App retrieves the local magnetic declination value to enable precise switching between true north and magnetic north. Location data is used only temporarily during the current session and is never persisted or uploaded. You may revoke location access at any time in system settings without affecting the compass's basic functionality.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Name, email address, or other personally identifiable information
  • Device unique identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, or other advertising IDs)
  • Usage habits, behavioral analytics, or telemetry data
  • Any data transmitted over the network

How We Use Information

The limited information we access (sensor data, optional location data) is used solely to deliver the App's core functionality. It is not used for any commercial, advertising, or analytics purpose.

Data Storage & Security

All data processing occurs locally on your device; the App does not send data to any external server. User preferences (e.g., true north enabled) are stored in the iOS/Android sandboxed storage, accessible only by this App and unreadable by other applications.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any of your information with third parties in any form.

Third-Party Services

Compass does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-tracking SDKs. The App is distributed through the Apple App Store or Google Play; your account and payment information is subject to Apple's or Google's respective privacy policies, which are independent of ours.

Your Rights

  • Revoke location permission: Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services at any time
  • Delete data: Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data from your device
  • Raise questions: Contact us at the address below

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has inadvertently provided personal information, please contact us immediately and we will address it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically due to business development or legal compliance requirements. Updates will be noted in the App Store release notes; significant changes will be announced at least 30 days in advance via in-app notification.

Governing Law

This policy is governed and interpreted in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.
