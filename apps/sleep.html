Sleep

白噪音助眠，告别失眠，拥有高质量睡眠。 White noise for better sleep — fall asleep faster and wake up refreshed.

关于 Sleep

Sleep 提供精心挑选的白噪音与自然音景，帮助你快速放松、屏蔽环境干扰，进入深度睡眠状态。所有音频内置于应用中，完全离线可用。

核心功能

  • 多种白噪音与自然音景（雨声、海浪、森林等）
  • 可自定义混音器，自由叠加多种音景
  • 定时关闭功能，到时自动淡出停止
  • 后台播放，锁屏后持续运行
  • 完全离线，无需网络连接
  • 无广告，无追踪

About Sleep

Sleep offers a carefully curated collection of white noise and nature soundscapes to help you relax, block out distractions, and drift into deep sleep. All audio is bundled with the app — no internet needed.

Key Features

  • Variety of white noise and nature sounds (rain, ocean, forest, and more)
  • Customizable mixer — layer multiple soundscapes freely
  • Sleep timer with automatic fade-out
  • Background playback — keeps running after screen lock
  • Fully offline, no internet connection required
  • No ads, no tracking
