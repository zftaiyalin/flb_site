About Compass

Compass is a clean, accurate compass app that uses your device's built-in sensors to give you reliable directional guidance. Whether you're hiking, navigating a city, or just curious which way is north, Compass gives you a clear, intuitive reading.

Key Features

  • High-precision magnetometer direction detection
  • Real-time degree and bearing display
  • One-tap switch between true north and magnetic north
  • Clean, minimal interface
  • No ads, no tracking
