概述

FLB Studio 旗下的应用通常无需注册账号即可使用。为了保存您的偏好设置、订阅状态等信息，应用会基于您的设备自动生成一个与设备绑定的用户标识（例如 Android 设备上的 Android ID）。本页面说明如何申请删除该标识所关联的全部数据。

如何申请删除数据

请发送邮件至 zftaifei@gmail.com，并在邮件中注明「数据删除请求」。为了能够准确定位并删除您的数据，请在邮件中提供以下信息：

  • App 名称：您使用的具体应用名称（如 Compass、Sleep 等）
  • 设备的 Android ID：可在「设置 > 关于手机 > 状态信息」或通过第三方设备信息工具查看；如不确定如何获取，可在邮件中说明，我们会协助您确认
  • （可选）订阅或购买凭证：如您购买过订阅或内购项目，请附上相关收据信息，便于我们核实并一并处理

删除范围

收到并核实您的请求后，我们将删除以下与该设备标识相关联的数据：

  • 与该 Android ID 绑定的本地化用户数据
  • 用户偏好与应用内配置信息
  • 与该标识关联的订阅 / 购买记录

数据保留说明

出于法律合规、防欺诈与对账需要，部分日志记录或支付相关凭证可能会在删除请求处理完成后继续保留 30 至 90 天，到期后将被自动清除，且不会再用于其他用途。

处理时间

我们将在收到您的删除请求后 30 天内完成处理，并通过邮件告知您处理结果。

联系我们

如对账号或数据删除有任何疑问，欢迎随时通过 zftaifei@gmail.com 与我们联系，我们通常会在 2 个工作日内回复。

Overview

FLB Studio apps generally do not require account registration. To remember your preferences and subscription status, an app automatically generates a device-bound user identifier (for example, the Android ID on Android devices). This page explains how to request deletion of all data associated with that identifier.

How to Request Data Deletion

Please email zftaifei@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." To help us locate and remove your data accurately, please include the following information:

  • App name: the specific app you use (e.g., Compass, Sleep)
  • Your device's Android ID: found under Settings > About Phone > Status Information, or via a third-party device info tool; if you're unsure how to find it, let us know and we'll help you confirm it
  • (Optional) Subscription or purchase proof: if you purchased a subscription or in-app item, attaching your receipt details helps us verify and process it together

What Gets Deleted

Once we receive and verify your request, we will delete the following data associated with your device identifier:

  • Locally stored user data tied to your Android ID
  • User preferences and in-app configuration
  • Subscription / purchase records linked to that identifier

Data Retention

For legal compliance, fraud prevention, and reconciliation purposes, certain logs or payment-related records may be retained for 30–90 days after your deletion request is processed, after which they are automatically purged and never used for any other purpose.

Processing Time

We will complete your deletion request within 30 days of receipt and notify you of the outcome by email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about account or data deletion, feel free to reach out at zftaifei@gmail.com. We typically respond within 2 business days.
